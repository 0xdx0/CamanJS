CamanJS is an attempt at providing a simple to use interface for dynamically manipulating images completely in Javascript. It strives to provide much of the basic functionality you would find in something like Photoshop. By this I mean, image contrast, brightness, levels, saturation, etc. At this time, something as complex as the Clone Tool is out of the question (but if you're feeling brave, be my guest).

CamanJS is also extensible and makes it very easy to add your own image manipulation plugins by abstracting over a lot of the actual canvas manipulation work.
